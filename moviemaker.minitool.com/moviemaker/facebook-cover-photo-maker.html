6 Best Online Facebook Cover Photo Makers

By Doreen | Follow | Last Updated May 17, 2021

Making a good Facebook cover photo is very important because it is the first photo seen by someone who visits your profile. How to create an amazing cover photo? Here are the top 6 websites for creating Facebook cover photos. If you want to make a Facebook cover video, please try MiniTool MovieMaker.

On This Page :
  • 1. Canva
  • 2. Adobe Spark
  • 3. Fotor
  • 4. Crello
  • 5. PicMonkey
  • 6. FotoJet
  • Bottom Line

Setting an attractive picture as your Facebook cover photo can help you leave a great impact on visitors. If you want to custom a great cover photo, here are the best 6 sites, designed for both beginners as well as professionals. All of them come with numerous template images for Facebook covers. Let’s check them out!

1. Canva

Canva is a graphic design platform, used to create social media graphics, presentations, posters, documents, videos, and other visual content. It is one of the best Facebook cover photo maker online sites, which comes with countless templates, elements, photos, texts, audio tracks, videos, etc. Everyone can create amazing Facebook covers with Canva.

2. Adobe Spark

Another great online Facebook cover photo maker is Adobe Spark. It is an integrated suite of media creation applications for mobile and web. With it, you can create impactful social graphics, logos, videos, banners, posters, slideshows, and much more in minutes with Adobe Spark. Better yet, you can start with a standard Facebook cover size template.

Also read: Top 6 Photo Stock Sites Like Shutterstock [Free & Paid]

3. Fotor

The next online Facebook cover photo maker is Fotor. It is a photo-editing platform, which comes with a wide range of stickers, texts, photo effects, and well-organized graphic design tools. Besides, it offers thousands of beautiful and professionally designed templates to meet your design needs. With Fotor, it would be much easier to create an awesome cover photo of your Facebook.

4. Crello

As the simplest online image editor, Crello can also be used as a Facebook cover photo maker. The site enables you to create your own designs, posts, covers, animations, etc. Additionally, it provides you with 50K+ professional graphic design templates, 60+ design formats for web and print, 32K+ animated objects, 180M+ images, videos, and audio tracks, as well as background remover.

5. PicMonkey

The fifth Facebook cover photo maker online site on the list is PicMonkey. It is an online photo editing and design service with 9,000+ graphics and textures, 100s of gorgeous fonts, 1000s of templates, stock photos, and videos. It is capable of creating beautiful photos, logos, and social media graphics. PicMonkey is a paid service, but it offers you a free trial.

Related article: Facebook Cover Videos: How to Make and Upload

6. FotoJet

The last online Facebook photo maker we’d like to introduce is FotoJet. It is a popular online service for graphic design, photo collage, and photo editing. The professional templates and resourceful stock make it simple to create posters, flyers, cards, invitations, social media graphics, banners, and much more. The best part of FotoJet is there is no registration required.

Note: Typically, a Facebook cover photo is 820 x 312 pixels on computers, while it is 640 x 360 pixels on mobile phones.
